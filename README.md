# Cart Coin Madness — Support

Cart Coin Madness is a low-poly arcade stunt driving game for iPhone and iPad. Drive through a sunset city, launch off ramps, spin and flip, land clean, and collect coins to chase your best score.

## Contact

For questions, feedback, or bug reports, email: 1@prismdatagroup.com

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

## Frequently Asked

**Is the game free?** Yes, Cart Coin Madness is free to download and play.

**Does the game need an internet connection?** No. The game is fully playable offline.

**Where is my best score saved?** Scores and settings are stored locally on your device.
