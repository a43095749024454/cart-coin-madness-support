# Privacy Policy — Cart Coin Madness

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Cart Coin Madness ("the app") is published by Prism Data Group. This policy explains what data the app handles.

## Data Collection

Cart Coin Madness does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

## Local Storage

Game progress — best scores, coin totals, and settings — is stored only on your device. It is never sent to us or to any third party.

## Game Center

If you sign in to Apple Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center profile and scores for leaderboards. That data is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy; we do not receive or store it on any server of our own.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page.
